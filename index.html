Opportunity

East West Gem Co. has disrupted the engagement industry with their beautiful, custom moissanite jewelry. Just as stunning as a diamond, their moissanite jewelry offers the same look, but with an affordable price tag for shoppers.

Since its founding, the brand has grown by bringing completely custom, dream rings to life with transparent pricing. By solely offering custom moissanite jewelry, East West Gem Co. has cultivated a customer base that knows they don’t have to sacrifice budget for beauty.

Characterized by an unwavering commitment to their customers, it was important to East West Gem Co. that each customer have the ability to comfortably wear and take home their completely custom, artisan-made piece.
